Strategic Recommendations

Based on the May 2025 data, here are actionable steps to enhance performance and maximize return on investment.

1. Enhance Conversion Tracking

Refine conversion value tracking to accurately reflect the actual revenue. For high-cost search campaigns, this is essential to accurately calculate ROI and justify ad spend.

2. Optimize Ad Creative

Pause or redesign high-click, zero-confirmed-registration assets. Systematically implement Google's "Ad Strength" recommendations to improve relevance, Quality Score, and ad rank.

3. Fix and Improve Landing Pages

Immediately investigate and fix the technical issues on the `/leasing` page. Conduct a UX audit of low-engagement pages like `/buy-or-lease-landing/` to better align content with ad messaging.

4. Re-evaluate Automated Campaigns

Analyze why Performance Max and Smart campaigns were paused. Consider reactivating them with optimized settings to leverage Google's automation for broader reach and efficiency.

5. Focus on High-Intent Keywords

Continue to invest in high-performing international search campaigns, like those targeting "IPv4 lease", as they attract highly qualified and valuable users, even with a higher CPC.

6. Implement Micro-Conversions

For Display & Demand Gen, track upper-funnel actions (e.g., content downloads, video views) to better measure their contribution to the customer journey beyond direct sales.
